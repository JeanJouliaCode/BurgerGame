As you can probably guess, BurgerMaster is a game about the exquisite art of cooking burgers. The ultimate goal is to make burgers as fast as possible. To achieve this goal, you will need to take action.
First thing first, the Kitchen. you can see each burger being prepared with care and love.
On the top are the ingredient needed to prepare the current command. The preparation won't start until your cook has all the ingredients required in the reserve.
On the bottom is displayed all the money you've earned, you will need it to hire chefs and get new ingredients as explained below.
More burger means more ingredients. The top right part of the screen is dedicated to your ingredient reserve. Each reserve gets filled periodically. The faster your chef is, the faster you will need ingredient. You can upgrade your reserve and increase the speed at which it get refilled by clicking on the button bellow. It will cost you more and more with each upgrade, but will also add 2 ingredients instantly!
The best burgers are made by the best chef. With each new chef hired, you will see the average height of your burger increase. It takes skill to stack ingredient that height! But that's not all, better chef are also quicker and the speed at which burgers gets prepared will significantly increase if the reserve can keep up. Better chef can also wield better tool. Unique accessories will be available with each new chef hired.
